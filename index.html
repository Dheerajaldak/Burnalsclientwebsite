About Us

Welcome To Bernal Lawn Services, Your Partner for a Beautiful Outdoor Space

At Bernal Lawn Services, we are dedicated to providing "Quality Service" for all your lawn and landscaping needs. With a passion for transforming outdoor spaces, Jose and our team offer a comprehensive range of services, including expert lawn care, grass and sod installation, elegant stone and metal edging, and efficient drainage systems. We specialize in creating stunning rock gardens, vibrant flowerbeds with mulch, and ensuring your irrigation is perfect with sprinkler repair. Our commitment extends to maintaining the health of your lawn through effective fertilizer and weed control. Let us help you cultivate the perfect outdoor oasis.
